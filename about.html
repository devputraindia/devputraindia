About Devputra Agro

Empowering villages through sustainable agriculture, rural entrepreneurship, and future-ready skills.

Who We Are

Devputra Agro is a rural empowerment initiative dedicated to helping farmers and villagers build sustainable livelihoods through modern agriculture, business guidance, and market access.

We work closely with rural communities to promote high-value crops, organic farming, and small-scale entrepreneurship—ensuring financial independence and long-term growth.

  • Promoting high-value and organic crop cultivation
  • Connecting farmers with buyers and partners
  • Providing modern farming knowledge & training
  • Supporting rural entrepreneurs and startups
  • Building strong village-level communities

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our mission and impact across rural India.

Trust & Transparency

We build honest relationships with farmers and partners.

Sustainable Growth

Farming methods that protect nature and ensure long-term income.

Knowledge for All

Accessible education for every villager, regardless of background.

Future-Ready Skills

About Devputra Agro

Empowering rural India through sustainable agriculture, training, and community upliftment.

Who We Are

Devputra Agro is a rural empowerment initiative dedicated to strengthening village economies through sustainable farming, business training, and modern agricultural practices.

We work closely with farmers, rural entrepreneurs, and youth to provide hands-on mentorship, crop guidance, and profitable pathways that create long-term stability.

  • Supporting villagers in cultivating high-value organic crops.
  • Connecting local farmers with national and global markets.
  • Helping rural producers sell handmade products across India.
  • Providing modern farming tools, training, and awareness.
  • Guiding rural entrepreneurs in building profitable businesses.

Our Mission

To empower rural communities through agricultural training, digital education, and entrepreneurship—creating financially strong and future-ready villages.

Our Vision

A future where every village grows sustainably, adopts modern farming, and creates opportunities for every household.

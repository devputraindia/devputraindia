About Devputra Agro

Devputra Agro is a rural empowerment initiative focused on helping villagers build sustainable businesses, grow high-value crops, and access modern agricultural knowledge. Our goal is to create independent, financially strong, and future-ready communities.

We work closely with farmers, rural entrepreneurs, and village youth, providing hands-on mentorship, training, and support so they can grow, earn, and create new opportunities within their own villages.

  • Supporting villagers in growing high-value organic crops.
  • Building a bridge between local producers and global buyers.
  • Helping villagers sell their handmade products worldwide.
  • Providing modern farming knowledge & training.
  • Guiding rural entrepreneurs with business planning.

Our Mission

To empower rural communities through digital skills, modern agriculture practices, and accessible education, building a foundation of growth, dignity, and opportunity.
